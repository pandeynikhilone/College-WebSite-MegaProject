SEMESTER 1

Discover the fundamentals of computing and programming with courses in computer basics, mathematical structures, and system design. Hands-on practice with C programming and minor projects will lay the groundwork for your technical skills.

SEMESTER 2

Dive into data structures, computer networks, and operating systems while enhancing your communication skills. Practical sessions in C++ and data structures will solidify your understanding of these essential concepts.

SEMESTER 3

Explore database management, Python programming, and computer architecture. Gain practical experience with DBMS and Python projects, and learn to analyze algorithms and organizational behavior.

SEMESTER 4

Learn about cloud computing, Java programming, and web designing. Engage in practical projects involving Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and delve into software engineering and numerical methods.

SEMESTER 5

Advance your skills in C#, mobile app development, and artificial intelligence. This semester includes practical work in C# and a qualifying summer internship to enhance your industry readiness.

SEMESTER 6

Focus on data warehousing, Linux, and network security. Complete your industrial training and project, and prepare for a qualifying research presentation or publication to showcase your expertise.
